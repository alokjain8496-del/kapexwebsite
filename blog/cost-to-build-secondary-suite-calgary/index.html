Cost to Build a Secondary Suite in Calgary (2026 Complete Guide)

March 27, 2026 Kapex Infra Team Calgary, Alberta
Secondary SuitesCost GuideCalgary PermitsBudgeting

Building a secondary suite in Calgary is one of the smartest ways to generate rental income and increase property value. But one of the first questions homeowners ask is simple: how much does it actually cost?

The answer depends on several factors, including the type of suite, size, construction requirements, and City approvals. In this guide, we break down realistic costs in Calgary so you can plan your budget with confidence.

What Is the Average Cost of a Secondary Suite in Calgary?

In Calgary, the cost of building a secondary suite varies depending on whether you are developing a basement suite, a garage suite, or legalising an existing unit.

Typical cost ranges:

  • ✔ Basement secondary suite: $40,000 – $80,000
  • ✔ Garage suite (detached): $100,000 – $250,000+
  • ✔ Legalizing an existing suite: $15,000 – $30,000

These numbers can vary depending on finishes, structural work, and permit requirements, but they provide a realistic starting point for most homeowners.

What Goes Into the Cost?

Many homeowners underestimate the full scope of costs involved. Building a legal secondary suite is not just about construction — it includes planning, compliance, and finishing.

Here’s what your budget typically covers:

  • ✔ Design and architectural drawings
  • ✔ Construction materials and labour
  • ✔ Plumbing and electrical work
  • ✔ Insulation and soundproofing
  • ✔ Kitchen and bathroom installation
  • ✔ Flooring, lighting, and finishes
  • ✔ City permits and inspections

Every component plays a role in ensuring your suite meets Calgary’s legal and safety standards.

Permit Costs and City Requirements in Calgary

To build a legal secondary suite, you must follow the regulations set by the City of Calgary. This includes zoning approval, building permits, and safety compliance.

Permit-related costs may include:

  • ✔ Development permit (if required)
  • ✔ Building permit fees
  • ✔ Electrical and plumbing permits
  • ✔ Inspection costs

Calgary Secondary Suite Incentives and Grants

The City of Calgary and the Government of Alberta occasionally offer incentive programs to encourage homeowners to develop legal secondary suites.

These programs may help offset:

  • ✔ Construction costs
  • ✔ Permit fees
  • ✔ Upgrades for code compliance

Because incentives change over time, it’s important to stay updated or work with a contractor who understands current programs.

Review available details in the Calgary secondary suite incentive program.

What Factors Affect the Final Cost?

No two projects are the same. Your final cost depends on several key factors:

  • ✔ Size of the suite
  • ✔ Basement vs detached garage suite
  • ✔ Structural changes required
  • ✔ Upgrading utilities (plumbing, electrical)
  • ✔ Quality of finishes and materials
  • ✔ Accessibility and layout complexity

For example, adding a completely new entrance or upgrading ceiling height can significantly impact your budget.

How to Reduce Your Secondary Suite Costs

The good news is that there are smart ways to control your budget without compromising quality.

  • ✔ Plan an efficient layout to reduce construction complexity
  • ✔ Use existing plumbing and electrical lines where possible
  • ✔ Choose durable but cost-effective finishes
  • ✔ Work with experienced contractors familiar with Calgary regulations

A well-planned project can save you thousands of dollars while still delivering a high-quality result.

Is Building a Secondary Suite Worth It?

For many Calgary homeowners, the answer is yes.

A secondary suite can:

  • ✔ Generate consistent rental income
  • ✔ Increase property value
  • ✔ Help offset mortgage costs
  • ✔ Provide flexible living space for family

Over time, the return on investment can make the initial construction cost worthwhile.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build a secondary suite in Calgary?

The cost typically ranges from $40,000 to $80,000 for a basement suite and $100,000 to $250,000+ for a garage suite. The final cost depends on size, design, and construction requirements.

Do I need a permit to build a secondary suite in Calgary?

Yes, you need proper permits from the City of Calgary, including development and building permits. Electrical and plumbing permits may also be required depending on the project.

Are there incentives available for secondary suites in Calgary?

Yes, the City of Calgary and Alberta government may offer incentive programs to support legal secondary suites. These can help reduce construction and compliance costs.

How long does it take to build a secondary suite?

Most secondary suite projects take between 8 to 16 weeks, depending on complexity, permits, and construction scope.

Is building a secondary suite a good investment in Calgary?

Yes, a secondary suite can generate rental income, increase property value, and help offset mortgage costs, making it a strong long-term investment.

Can I legalize an existing basement suite in Calgary?

Yes, existing suites can be legalized by upgrading them to meet current building and safety codes. This usually costs between $15,000 and $30,000.
